About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating hordes of creatures that become increasingly difficult.

As you progress, you'll unlock powerful abilities and weapons to aid your survival. Collect experience points to level up and strengthen your character. The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes.

With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Monster Survivors keeps you on the edge of your seat as you strive to beat your previous records and climb the leaderboards.

Survival Tips

  1. Keep moving: Standing still is a death sentence. Always stay on the move to avoid being surrounded.
  2. Prioritize enemies: Some monsters are more dangerous than others. Learn to identify threats and eliminate them first.
  3. Collect all experience gems: Every bit of experience counts. Don't leave any gems behind.
  4. Choose upgrades wisely: Think about your playstyle when selecting upgrades. Some combinations work better than others.
  5. Use obstacles: The environment can be your ally. Use obstacles to funnel enemies or create space.
