About the Game

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping survival game that tests your skills against endless hordes of monsters. Your mission is simple yet challenging: survive as long as possible while battling increasingly difficult waves of creatures.

As you progress, you'll unlock powerful abilities, collect upgrades, and develop strategies to overcome the monstrous challenges. Each playthrough is unique, offering endless replayability and excitement.
