About the Game

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping survival game where you battle against endless hordes of monsters. Your mission is simple yet challenging: survive as long as possible while defeating increasingly difficult waves of creatures.

As you progress, you'll unlock powerful abilities, collect experience points, and upgrade your character. The longer you survive, the more intense the challenge becomes.
